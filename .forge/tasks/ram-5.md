Let a household set a monthly spending cap per category, with a warning at ~80% and a clear "over budget" state at 100%.

**Why:** HOMU captures transactions well but nothing *guides* spending. Categories + reports exist; a budget closes the loop. Biggest missing piece for the family use-case.

**Design-heavy** — new screen(s). Worth mocking visually first: budget setup, the progress bars on the reports/home view, and the over-budget treatment.

Open questions for design: per-category vs. overall budget, where the warning surfaces (home card? category row?), rollover behavior.
